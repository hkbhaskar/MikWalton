Contract to Hire

A hybrid of temporary and direct hire, contract-to-hire is a way for clients and talent to test the waters. It allows potential employees to prove themselves to the company and see if the culture is a good fit. It is a side door into an organization.
Likewise, it allows the client to test the candidate for a predetermined period of time and see if him/her fits well into their system. It is also Budget friendly. MW partners up with the best firms and potential resources globally to help both - its client and candidates keep up with the varying business and industry needs.
